Work in Progress

Minerva does the following:

  • Scrape titles of all front page articles of the top 10 UK Newspapers*
  • Find the most common words in the titles
  • Exclude words that are common in the English language (e.g. "is", "the", "had")
  • Visualise word frequency trends

*:BBC, The Guardian, The Independent, The Telegraph, Daily Mail, The Sun, Mirror Online, Express>
